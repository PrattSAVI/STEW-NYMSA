The Stewardship Mapping and Assessment (STEW-MAP) project visualizes groups that help take care of the environment, or stewards, on a digital map. Our last survey in NYC closed in 2018, resulting in a mapped database. Help us keep groups' contact information current - and include new groups or groups that haven't yet taken the survey. Below, you can find and edit already mapped groups, by looking at the list on the right - or add a new group by clicking "Click here to add a group to the map". Your participation in STEW-MAP is completely voluntary. At a later stage, groups added to this map may be contacted for the full STEW-MAP survey, as part of an update.
